A Brief
Introduction...

Settled on the banks of Ibar and Sitnica rivers.
The city is one of the oldest known settlements in Kosovo.
It became an industrial town, formerly the economic centre of Kosovo because of the nearby Trepča Mines.

Places
to Visit

Below are some of the most visited places in Mitrovica.

Sheshi "Mehe Uka" ("Korza")

Here is where most of the people come to relax or take a walk. You can find many caffes,bakeries,shops and restaurants. You can also buy some street snacks.There is often a small library on the side.

The artificial lake

This is a more recent addition.Biking,running,swiming are some of the preferred activities.(There's a pool nearby aswell.) You can enjoy the murals done by the youth of Mitrovica, as well as try some of the restaurants there.

The Olympic Statium "Adem Jashari"

If you are a sports person you might be lucky enough to catch a game here.
It has a capacity of 18,500.
It has been renovated a few times.

A few more...

The bridge

This is the bridge that divides the city into the northern and southern parts.

The Crystal Museum

Trepca is one of the most famous mines in Balkan. It has been the pride of Mitrovica for many years. In the museum you can see different crystals from there.
